Our Story

Built on Trust, Powered by Technology

GoSafe Global was founded in 2009 with a single mission: to make India safer. Starting with a small range of walk-through metal detectors, we have grown into one of India's most comprehensive security equipment companies.

Today we serve 500+ organizations — from international airports to local schools — supplying over 65 categories of security, safety and access control products. Every product we sell is rigorously tested, ISO certified and backed by our dedicated after-sales team.
